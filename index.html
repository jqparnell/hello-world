Hello, World...

...my name is Jacqueline Parnell! I have been a digital artist for about a decade.
I love music, drawing, archeology, astronomy, coding, and crafting.
From an early age, I hung around on the internet, practicing safety and staying in my lane.
I have always had an interest in scripting and coding.
The internet feels like home already, so learning how to build within it feels appropriate.

Selections from CWD Index

  • Fire

    My first choice is this animation work called Fire, which has a roaring fire at the
    bottom of the screen, and creates a trail of fire wherever you click.
    It's fun to do elegant patterns.

  • Geo Kitten

    My second choice is the online work called Geo Kitten, which takes you through
    a series of kitten photos that "reveal your location".
    The actual way it knows your location is through IP address.

  • Native Land

    My final choice is the dynamic work called Native Land, which is an app that
    maps Indigenous territories, treaties, and languages.
    I find this very useful, and wish it was more widespread.
